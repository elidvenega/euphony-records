About

Euphony records was founded because of my love for music and wanting to provide music records of different genres to people, decided to open a record store. We host local acts on weekends to give them exposure, as well sponsor many community events as a way to give back to the community. Our mission is just to expose a new generation to how music was back in the older days and give them a chance to experience that.
